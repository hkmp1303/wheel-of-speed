# **Game Rules**

Players win currency by being the first to correctly guess the word.
The value for each round is determined by the prize wheel. The player who starts the round spins the wheel. The value for any given word does not change at any point. For each new round there is a new word and a new prize amount.

The target word is slowly revealed as one player makes guesses. The guess is considered incorrect if the word is incomplete. The first letter is revealed after the wheel has spun, a value locked and the timer has started. The player then has 5-10 seconds before each letter is revealed. Players win the round and gain the reward after correctly guessing the target word. A player can make as many guesses as time allows until the word is correctly identified. Timer lenght is dependent on word length. 

If the player is unsuccessful, their opponent will have 20 seconds to guess and win the reward. Each player's turn begins with a new word and a new spin to determine the reward amount.

No currency is gained or lost by either player if the word is not identified before the final letter is revealed. The player that gains the most currency after four rounds wins the game.
